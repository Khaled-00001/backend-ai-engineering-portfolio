# What Are You Proving?

## Proof Statement

My primary strength is building reliable backend systems that solve real problems using Java, Spring Boot, SQL, and AI-powered workflows. I want to demonstrate this to backend engineering hiring managers and technical recruiters looking for interns or junior engineers who can contribute to production-ready projects. The one action I want them to take is to review my projects and invite me for a technical interview, where I can demonstrate both my problem-solving skills and my ability to build maintainable backend applications.

## Why This Needs to Exist

A CV and LinkedIn profile can list my skills, but they cannot prove that I can design, build, and deliver real backend systems with clean architecture, scalable APIs, and practical AI integration.
